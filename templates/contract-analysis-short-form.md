# Contract Analysis Template - Short Form

Use this template for concise contract analysis presentations. Focus on strategic insights and actionable intelligence.

## Template Structure

### Thinking Section (Narrative Analysis)
```xml
<thinking>
Provide strategic narrative analysis here. Focus on:
- What patterns emerge from the contract data
- Strategic implications for the business
- Risk assessment reasoning
- Opportunity identification logic
- Recommended prioritization rationale

Avoid data dumps - synthesize insights into coherent business narrative.
</thinking>
```

### Contract Evolution Summary
```markdown
## Contract Evolution

- **Current Status**: [Active/Under Review/Pending Renewal]
- **Evolution Path**: [Brief description of contract lifecycle stage]
- **Next Milestone**: [Upcoming transition or decision point]
```

## Usage Guidelines

### For Individual Contracts:
- Lead with strategic narrative in thinking section
- Emphasize business impact over technical details
- Prioritize actionable insights
- Connect contract specifics to broader business objectives

### For Multiple Contracts:
- Synthesize patterns across agreements
- Identify cross-contract optimization opportunities
- Highlight portfolio-level risks and opportunities
- Recommend strategic prioritization

### Key Principles:
1. **Strategic First**: Lead with business implications
2. **Action-Oriented**: Every insight should suggest next steps
3. **Prioritized**: Clearly indicate what matters most
4. **Concise**: Maximum 2-3 pages for short form
5. **Narrative Thinking**: Use thinking section for strategic reasoning
