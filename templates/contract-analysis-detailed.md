# Contract Analysis Template - Detailed Form

Use this template for comprehensive contract analysis with full data extraction and strategic assessment.

## Template Structure

### Executive Thinking (Strategic Narrative)
```xml
<thinking>
Provide comprehensive strategic analysis addressing:

**Business Context Analysis**:
- How does this contract fit within the broader business strategy?
- What market conditions or business needs drove this agreement?
- How does this agreement compare to industry standards?

**Risk and Opportunity Assessment**:
- What are the primary risk vectors and their business impact?
- Where are the growth opportunities and how can they be leveraged?
- What are the competitive implications of this contract structure?

**Portfolio Integration**:
- How does this contract interact with other agreements?
- What dependencies or conflicts exist with parallel contracts?
- What optimization opportunities exist at the portfolio level?

**Strategic Recommendations**:
- What actions will maximize business value from this agreement?
- How should resources be allocated to manage this contract?
- What are the long-term strategic implications?

Focus on synthesis and strategic reasoning rather than data recitation.
</thinking>
```

## Usage Guidelines

### For Complex Contracts:
- Use detailed thinking to explain strategic context
- Extract all available CLM structured data
- Provide comprehensive tabular analysis
- Focus on long-term strategic implications

### For Portfolio Analysis:
- Synthesize patterns across multiple contracts
- Identify optimization opportunities at portfolio level
- Recommend resource allocation strategies
- Highlight cross-contract dependencies

### Key Principles:
1. **Strategic Depth**: Use thinking section for comprehensive strategic analysis
2. **Data-Rich**: Include all relevant structured data and tables
3. **Actionable Intelligence**: Every data point should inform decision-making
4. **Comprehensive**: Cover all aspects of contract lifecycle and business impact
5. **Narrative Integration**: Weave data into strategic business narrative
